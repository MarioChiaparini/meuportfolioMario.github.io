Biography

Olá, meu nome é Mario!

I am a student of chemical engineering of the seventh period, with which loves technologies and the newest trends of the century like IoT (Internet of Things) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). So in my second year of college I already started my great journey in the world of technology, starting with being a student of embedded systems (Raspberry Pi, ESP32, ESP8266, Orange PI and Pyboards) geared towards internet of things applications and embedded machine learning. Later from learning languages ​​like Python and C/C++ to applications like AI and the internet of things.
With all these stacks a startup in my city (Indaiatuba) decided give me an opportunity to work on one of the Internet of Things projects for the company HUMMEL+MANN, where numerous boards were manufactured (own hardware) and programmed in Python where I was able to perform the interconnection between the filters and the internet.
I just finished an extension course from Stanford Continuing Studies, on machine learning, which helped me a lot in the updates of stacks focused on the creation of neural networks for artificial intelligence. Well, at the moment I am studying javascript applied to artificial intelligence and in the creation of machine learning models using the Brain.js library and for creating backend of these applications I am learning node js.
